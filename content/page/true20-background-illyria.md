#  Citizen of Illyria 
Illyria's independence from Narin rule was born from sword and fire. The Sun-Prophet Morcuru liberated the people of Illyria from tyranny, and to tyranny they shall never return. An Illyrian's sacred duty is to exceed themselves whenever possible, to constantly push their own limits, to 'burn brighter'. They follow the example of their Heirophant, the mystical scion of a divine bloodline. While this ambition might seem to some as folly, their zeal gives the citizens of Illyria an undeniable appeal.
 * ''Ability Adjustments:'' +1 Charisma, -1 Wisdom.
* ''Bonus Feats:'' Citizens of Illyria gain one bonus feat at 1st level out of the list of feats available for the hero's role.
* ''Bonus Skill:'' Citizens of Illyria gain one bonus known skill at 1st level, in addition to those gained for the hero's role and Intelligence score.
* ''Favored Feats:'' Jack-of-All-Trades, Seize Initiative
