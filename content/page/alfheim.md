In the east of the southern continent, there is a great forest of pine, known
to the Mannaz as Alfheim, or Land of Elves. Few among the Mannaz even speak of
such a place, for fear of invoking the wrath of the fey who live in that sacred
wood, for not only Elves dwell there, but Pixies, Brownies, and Spriggans also.
It is a mystical place, and unfit for human eyes.
