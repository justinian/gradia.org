The Shining One, Lightbringer, Lord of Morning, Fist of Light, these are all names for the Sun King, the great god of the Sun. His is possibly the most influencial church in the Western Reaches, which temples in every major city, and shrines on most major roads. His powers are great, for there are few things in the world which the sun does not touch, and those things wisely hide from the Sun King's wrath.

Worshippers of the Sunlord channel positive energy, as the sun's gift is life. In the Eastern Sands there are rumors of cults of sun worshippers who channel death energies, but there are no such cults in the West. Clergy of the Sunlord have titles based on their position in the heirarchy, and no general title is given, though 'Father' or 'Mother' is customary, and many Questing Knights choose to be called 'Bright One'.

The Sun King is most often described as a powerful warrior, in a golden breast plate, a round shield of bronze, and weilding the Rising Sun, a flaming morning star of impossible brightness. Some have described him as a gentle youth, with a healing hand, and others as an old man, wise and proud.

Followers of the Sun King learn his teachings of glory, honor, and mercy, usually in that order. Their Questing Priests travel the land, seeking out evil and darkness and banishing it from the world. They are often proud, and can be boastful, emulating the warrior aspect of their god. A smaller cross section of the church of the Sun King chooses mercy first, making healing the focus of their studies and practices. Such priests are welcome where ever they go, and are largely responsible for the impressive public support worshipers of the Sun King enjoy.

Domains: Sun, Fire, Healing, Good, War

Favored Weapon: Rising Sun (+2 Flaming Burst Morningstar of Impact)
