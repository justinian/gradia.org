#  Citizen of Rygalta 
Rygaltans are a proud people, and rightly so. Their civilization has survived, largely unchanged, for hundreds, perhaps thousands, of years. They consider themselves the birthplace of knowledge, and few, if any, other cultures now surviving can make such a boast. Though learned, the people of the Shining City are hardy and strong. They fight a never-ending war against Xar incursion, and have repelled numerous crusades from the Shattered Empire. Even their daily life is conducted under an unrelenting sun that would wither other people.
 * ''Ability Adjustments:'' None.
* ''Bonus Feats:'' Tireless, Weapon Training
* ''Bonus Skill:'' Citizens of Rygalta gain one bonus known skill at 1st level, in addition to those gained for the hero's role and Intelligence score.
* ''Favored Feats:'' Favored Opponent (Xar), Fascinate
