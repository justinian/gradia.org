[Animal Handling]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/animal-handling.md" >}})
 The keeping and training of animals. Can be used to train a wild horse, to herd cattle, or to train a wild beast. Complexity can reflect the difficulty of the animal to handle (from dog to tiger), or the difficulty of the task being trained (from 'Sit' to carrying a message to a specific person).
[Archery]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/archery.md" >}})
 The use and maintainance of projectile weapons, such as bows and crossbows. Complexity can reflect the difficulty of the weapon (shortbow, longbow, crossbow), or the difficulty of the shot (from simple aiming to a disarming bank shot).
[Athletics]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/athletics.md" >}})
 Physical feats like sprinting and climbing fall into the category. Complexity can reflect a game's elaborate rules, or use of tools to aid in the task (such as rappelling or snorkelling).
[Business]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/business.md" >}})
 The management of financial resources, including appraising and selling goods. Complexity can reflect exotic markets or bureaucratic entanglements.
[Camaraderie]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/camaraderie.md" >}})
 The ability to make others comfortable in casual social situations and build familiarity and friendships. Complexity can reflect the guardedness of the target or social differences.
[Casting]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/casting.md" >}})
 The control of mystical forces to generate effects external to the caster. Complexity can reflect the range, duration, area of effect or potency of the effect. The character must have a Casting [Perk]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/magic/perks-and-flaws.md" >}}) to possess this skill.
[Channeling]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/channeling.md" >}})
 Channeling an external entity or spiritual concept to gain additional abilities. This can be used for shapeshifting, or adopting the persona of an heroic ancestor. Complexity can reflect the scope of change (gaining a cat's eyes versus turning into a cat) or the amount of power to be channeled (a minor power versus a godlike power). The character must have a Channeling [Perk]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/magic/perks-and-flaws.md" >}}) to possess this skill.
[Combat Sense]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/combat-sense.md" >}})
 Awareness of violent intentions and ability to react quickly in combat situations. Complexity can reflect the scope of the conflict (from a duel to a battle), as well as knowledge of tactics and strategems.
[Conjuring]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/skills/conjuring.md" >}})
 The ability to create and control [chimera]({{< relref "chimera.md" >}}) or [ephemera]({{< relref "ephemera.md" >}}). This can be used to 'summon' seemingly living, often fantastical creatures, or to create useful objects from thin air. Complexity reflects the power of a summoned creature or the intricacy of the object. The character must have a Conjuring [Perk]({{< relref "silhouette-rules/magic/perks-and-flaws.md" >}}) to possess this skill.
