|| School || Evocation ||
|| Duration || up to MOS rounds of concentration ||
|| Effect || 10 x MOS m ||
|| Availability || Divinity/2 ||

The caster creates a gust of wind in a straight line. Objects and creatures in its path must succeed at a BLD test (target 2+ spell MOS) or be knocked down and thrown a number of meters equal to MOF. If successful, the creature can only advance a number of meters equal to the MOS on the BLD test. Lateral movement is uneffected.

Additionally, missile attacks that intersect the effect of the spell recieve a penalty equal to the spell MOS.
