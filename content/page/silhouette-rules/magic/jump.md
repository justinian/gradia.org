|| School || Transmutation ||
|| Duration || Instant ||
|| Effect || Caster ||
|| Availability || Divinity/2 ||

The caster can leap incredible heights and distances. With a successful roll, the caster adds MOS meters to his/her vertical jump, and MOS x2 meters to his/her horizontal jump. This spell can also cushion a fall from up to MOS x2 meters.
