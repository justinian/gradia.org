|| School || Divine Air ||
|| Duration || Concentration ||
|| Effect || caster ||
|| Availability || Divinity/1 ||

This spell creates a strong wind behind the caster that increases his/her speed. The caster adds his/her MOS to his/her fitness for determining movement speed (or 5 x MOS in meters to Sprint speed). 

The caster can effect other targets, but each additional target adds 1 to the complexity.
