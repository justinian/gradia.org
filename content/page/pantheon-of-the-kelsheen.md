##  The Allasheen 
**Oddamura the Warrior**

Oddamura the Warrior is worshipped by the tribes of the Kelsheen as one half of the ''Allasheen''.  He is revered as the father of the Kelsheen, a strong warrior and defender of his children. He is always depicted alongside his wife, Almira the Wanderer.  He is a centaur-like being, with the upper-body of a man and the lower body of a horse.  His hair is a mane of sunlight that flows across the sky.

Portfolio: Strength, Courage, Horses, Family, Battle, Day

Domains: Animal, Strength, Sun, War

Weapon: Shortspear
 * *Almira the Wanderer**

Almira the Wanderer is worshipped by the tribes of the Kelsheen as one half of the ''Allasheen''.  She is revered as the mother of the Kelsheen, a cunning traveler and defender of her children. She is always depicted alongside her husband, Oddamura the Warrior.  She is a centaur-like being, with the upper-body of a woman and the lower body of a horse.  Her hair is a mane of stars that flows across the sky.

Portfolio: Travel, Family, Horses, Protection, Fortune, Night

Domains: Animal, Luck, Travel, Protection

Weapon: Bow (longbow or shortbow)
