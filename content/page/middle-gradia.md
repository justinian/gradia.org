Middle Gradia is marked above all by the [Empire of Narin]({{< relref "empire-of-narin.md" >}}). At some point during its long and rich history, Narin has controlled almost all of the human-controlled lands in the middle regions of the continent. One notable example is the city of [Traemon]({{< relref "traemon.md" >}}) on the western coast - the city has been under Narin control three times.

The wide mountain ranges of Middle Gradia also house the Dwarven cities of the [Nauglimir]({{< relref "nauglimir.md" >}}). And to the east, the great forested lands are home to the great Elven city of [Eindonallan]({{< relref "eindonallan.md" >}}).
