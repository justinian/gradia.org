Frost bitten forests, snowcapped mountains, castles of ice... the land of the
southern continent is another world...

The southern continent is south of the Rookspine Mountains, and is, for all
intents, cut off from the North. It is a frozen land, cold even in summer. In
Winter, it snows for weeks on end, blanketing everything in a sheet of
shimmering white.

There is much to see in the south, if one is strong enough to survive there.
