''Traemon'' is the largest city between [Narin]({{< relref "empire-of-narin.md" >}}) and [Thalarion]({{< relref "thalarion.md" >}}) on Gradia's western coast. It is an important trading port for the western reaches, especially since it's the biggest mainland trading hub for the [Mar'shandi]({{< relref "mar-shandi.md" >}}).
