|| Age || Approx. 1000 years ||
|| Location || Molitor's tomb ||
|| Created by || [Molitor]({{< relref "/molitor.md" >}}) ||
|| Children || None ||
* *Appearance**

Despite having great power, being one of Molitor's four
original creations, the years have not been kind to
Bhaa-enathlar. He appears as an older, dark-skinned man
with white hair and a longish beard. He is continuously
dressed in decaying robes.
* *Notes**

Bhaa-enathlar was the first disciple of Molitor. He,
along with Nthalogoth, took the directions of Molitor
most literally, and has remained - awake and conscious,
no less - in Molitor's tomb since the time in which
Molitor imprisioned himself. A strong wizard in life,
Bhaa-enathlar has spent his unlife since Molitor's
entombment continuing his magical studies and gaining in
power. He has placed strong wards around the tomb, and
even on the lands surrounding it, and uses strong
divination magics to continue his studies. With their
years of study, Bhaa-enathlar and Nthalogoth, while not
the most powerful, are certainly the most learned mages
in the world.
