**Appearance**

Raei-lel is the youngest of Molitor's "second generation" disciples, haven been
changed some 600 or so years after Molitor's entombment. He appears as an older
Rygaltan, often dressed in the garb of the position he has taken up lately as
Councillor and King's Advisor.

**Notes**

Raei-lel, while only 400 years old, was a very powerful sorcerer in life. He
has spent his years learning to combine his socerous power with the power he's
gained through undeath, and has achieved amazing results.
