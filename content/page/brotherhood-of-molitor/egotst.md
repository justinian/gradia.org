**Description**

Egotst appears as a younger middle-aged man with blonde hair and fairer skin
than his other bretheren. He doesn't seem to have a preferred style of dress,
instead going for the style of whatever he is currently involved in.

**Notes**

The youngest disciple of Molitor, Egotst split from [Bhaa-enathlar]({{< relref
"bhaa-enathlar.md" >}}) and [Nthalogoth]({{< relref "nthalogoth.md" >}}) with
[Aul'othaus]({{< relref "aul-othaus.md" >}}) to make sure Molitor's tomb would
never be found. He parted ways with [Aul'othaus]({{< relref "aul-othaus.md"
>}}) when they reached the mountains, and traveled south, knowing that the
northern populations were not the only ones that would some day be a threat to
Molitor's tomb.
