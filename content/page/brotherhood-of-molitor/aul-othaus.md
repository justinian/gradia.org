**Appearance**

Aul'othaus appears as a darker-skinned man in his prime, with salt-and-pepper
hair kept well-groomed, and expensive clothing.

**Notes**

Aul'othaus, the third disciple of Molitor, took a more liberal interpretation
of Molitor's last wishes.  Convincing [Egotst]({{< relref "egotst.md" >}}) to
follow him, Aul'othaus traveled north to the more populous lands of Men, and
set about to ensure that Molitor's tomb remained hidden.
