**Appearance and Notes**

The second disciple of Molitor, Nthalogoth has continued to be almost identical
to [Bhaa-enathlar]({{< relref "bhaa-enathlar.md" >}}) in power, and as the
many years have passed entombed with his "brother," Nthalogoth and
[Bhaa-enathlar]({{< relref "bhaa-enathlar.md" >}}) have grown increasingly
similar in most other aspects, as well.
