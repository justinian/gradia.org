The vast green of the Northern Plains is home to many creatures not found anywhere else in Gradia. The nomadic Kelsheen exiles dwell in this vast grassland, endlessly wandering from watering hole to watering hole. The traditions of this wild group varies from tribe to tribe, and their migrations rarely overlap. When they do, celebration or battle invariably occurs.

After generations of exile, very little of the Kelsheen's culture has remained unchanged. One thing that seems to have stayed is the tribal priests or 'skin dancers'. These shamans of the Kelsheen channel the Old Gods of the Kelsheen, totemic animal spirits that grant their forms and abilities to their hosts.
