"The gods may listen to a strong voice. They harken better to many strong voices in unison, some say. From the Church of the Luminous to the Fists of the Tyrant, from the Grove Keepers of the Elves to the Doombringers of Nehrull, religious orders can exist anywhere there are those who worship. Most are small, isolated cults, but some can grow into sprawling institutions that weild as much political might as they do divine magic.

"I know of many cults, and I can tell you stories to freeze your blood, or give you hope. But beware. There are few things in the world as dangerous as a zealot wronged."
