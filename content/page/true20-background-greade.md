#  Citizen of Greade 
The people of Greade have ever dwelt between civilization and chaos. To the north of the Greadan Uplands lays the Weirdlands, the roiling mystical blight where nightmares become real. As though in defiance of this horror, the people of Greade are nearly unbreakable, in mind and body. They revel in sports and tests of endurance, often rewarding those who show the greatest tolerance for suffering. Some among them venture into the Weirdlands and learn its ways. 
 * ''Ability Adjustments:'' +1 Constitution, -1 Charisma
* ''Bonus Feats:'' Talented (Athletics and Survival), Endurance
* ''Bonus Skill:'' Citizens of Greade gain one bonus known skill at 1st level, in addition to those gained for the hero's role and Intelligence score.
* ''Favored Feats:'' Diehard, Toughness
