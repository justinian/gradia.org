#  Craft 
**Ability**: Intelligence
* *Specialized**

The Craft skill allows players to work raw materials into useful or valuable goods. A Craft skill must have an associated specialization, representing the type of material with which the player is working. This skill can be selected multiple times, with each specialization being a separate skill.

Example Specializations:
* Metalworking
* Clothworking
* Woodworking
* Stoneworking
* Leatherworking
* Clayworking

Crafting an item is a two part process. The first step is designing the item, which typically involves rolling a Knowledge skill with an appropriate specialization (Knowledge: Engineering, Knowledge: Warfare) but other skills may be used (Sabotage for a lock, Survival for a trap) where appropriate. A good plan adds a bonus to the craft check. A poorly conceived plan or total improvisation results in a penalty on the craft check.
