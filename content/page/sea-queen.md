The Sea Queen is known by many names and faces. She is the tempestuous stormy sea, the steady but churning river, or the life-bringing rain. Trickery, emotion, and magic all fall under her domain. She is called Mari, The Lady of Magic, or even the Sea Witch. She is one of the gods worshiped in the pantheon of [Imperial Orthodoxy]({{< relref "imperial-orthodox.md" >}}), but is also revered on her own by many western cultures, especially coastal ones. What few religious people there are in [Thalarion]({{< relref "thalarion.md" >}}) call her the goddess of that city.
