  <th>Description</th>
  <td>Described by many as a young man with bright yellow-blond hair, and a strong build but gentle hand, Peylor is the god of the Sun, and of Light. He often appears as a young boy, 10-12 years of age, shortly after the winter solstice, and as an older middle-aged man before the solstice. He is a firm supporter, and often patron, of all other good-aligned gods, as well as most non-evil nature gods. Peylor's chosen on Gradia is usually The Hierophant (hence that person's position within the church), but he has been known to select people completely outside the Church when he has deemed appropriate.</td>
