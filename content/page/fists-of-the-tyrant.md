Though their rightful name is the Brotherhood of Hextor, they are better known
as the Fists of the Tyrant, for they are known less for their religion that
their actions. Often selling themselves as mercenaries, these warriors delight
in the scent of blood, the sight of a carrion strewn battlefield, and the feel
of scepter held in a gauntleted fist. It is said that one does not seek out the
Fists, they find you. They are known to make their services available  whenever
the stakes are great enough, but the cost of their aid is not counted in gold,
for often the only ones who end up victorious are the Fists. Though, that is
just rumor...
