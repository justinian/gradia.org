#  Citizen of Corin 
The kingdom of Corin has long been the cultural hub of the Shattered Empire. The craftsmanship and simple elegance of Corin goods are a product of a culture that prizes beauty and grace above all else, except possibly freedom. The government of Corin is a feudal monarchy, but the social expectations between the nobility and their subordinates engender respect, loyalty, and compassion both above and below. A citizen of Corin may become a hero for the poetic thrill, or simply because it is what the people need.
 * ''Ability Adjustments:'' None.
* ''Bonus Feats:'' Skill Focus (Choose one Craft or Perform specialization), Lucky
* ''Bonus Skill:'' Citizens of Corin gain one bonus known skill at 1st level, in addition to those gained for the hero's role and Intelligence score.
* ''Favored Feats:'' Inspire, Improvise Tools
