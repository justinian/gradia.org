The Silhouette version of Gradia has some major differences when compared to
the D20 rules. We feel the Silhouette system is more in tune with the flexible
nature of Gradia's magic system, and better represents a gritty, dangerous
world.

While Silhouette is a fairly developed rules system, there are aspects of the
game that require clarification, new rules, or revisions of existing rules.
