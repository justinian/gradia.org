The Order of Lucient, better known to the masses as the ''Red Guard'', are likely the most feared military force in known Gradia. Originally a small order of knights that served as elite palace guard for the Emperor of [Narin]({{< relref "empire-of-narin.md" >}}), the Order has grown vastly over the last couple centuries. The Order is now a standard compliment to Narin military units; they are the elite that even the officers of the Narin military pay heed to.

It is best to be wary of crossing a member of the Red Guard, for every one truly lives up to the reputation of the Order. The uniform of the Order on the battlefield is that of black armor - often of a frightening visage - with a long but thin crimson surcoat worn atop the armor. In situations where armor is less appropriate, the uniform of the Order is said to be all black with a crimson sash worn across the chest.
