Nyrdall is a mountain fortress and prison in the south of [Illyria]({{< relref "illyria.md" >}}). It was long used to hold criminals against the state and other high-profile prisoners in the days when Illyria was a part of the [Empire_of_Narin]({{< relref "empire-of-narin.md" >}}). After Illyria gained its freedom from Narin, Nyrdall fell vacant, until it was taken again by Narin as a stepping-off point for its operation to retake Illyria. Nyrdall once again fell vacant after the campaign was a success, until shortly before the invasion of [Corin]({{< relref "corin.md" >}}), when it became a stronghold for the [Red Guard]({{< relref "order-of-lucient.md" >}}).

#  Rumors 
Many rumors surround the mountain fortress, claiming that Nyrdall was originally a fortress of Dwarven origin (possibly part of the [Nauglimir]({{< relref "nauglimir.md" >}})), but that it was overtaken by demons or supernatural beings. They claim that this is the reason Nyrdall has never been held for a significant length of time by men. The fact that no one has held on to Nyrdall since the Red Guard occupied it goes a long way to help this rumor, and there could easily be some truth to the rumors of the Dwarven origin of the fortress.
