Ruins and ancient sites in the hills of the Jotunheim bear the mark of Vodn, an ancient god forgotten by the Mannaz and left to the ravages of time. Only scholars and sages learned in forgotten lore now recall the Prophecies of Vodn, which speak of his return from death to battle the god Fetahar, who stands over the gates of Mannahal (or paradise), and lets none enter.

Vodn, it is said, will return from death to throw down the armies of Fetahar at the end of the world, and allow the souls of the dead, even those corrupted by the fear of death, twisted into the Craven. Vodn will raise his spear and smite the Deathlord, and open the gates of Mannahal. He is a god of strength, war, vengeance, and the honored dead.

Domains: **War**, Chaos, Good, Retribution, Death

Favored Weapon: Fanignir (+2 Flaming Returning Shortspear of Distance)
