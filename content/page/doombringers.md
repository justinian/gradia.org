A death cult formed, it seems, in the deserts of the East, the Doombringers are
fanatics of the worst kind, with little to redeem them. They believe that death
is the ultimate state of existance, and life is a trick, a trap that binds us
to this world, keeping us from paradise. They do not simply kill themselves to
save all others the trouble, though I have heard tales of their initiation
ceremonies, stories of what happens to those who lack the conviction to lead
all mortalkind into the next world.

They seek the 'unfettering' of all living things. They stay in this world to be
shepards. I pity the sheep who would follow them.
