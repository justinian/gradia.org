#  Citizen of Thalarion 
A citizen of Thalarion need not be a student of the arcane arts to pick up a thing or two about magic. While the academic atmosphere of the City of Wonders ensures an enlightened civic government, the amount of raw power wielded by some of its citizenry leads the people of Thalarion to be cautious, courteous, and thoughtful. Thalarion is a meritocracy, where what you know and what you can do are far more important than where you come from. As a result, the city is a safe haven for the disenfranchised and exiled. Not all have what it takes to make it in Thalarion, and the slums are packed full of the disillusioned failures of all nations.
 * ''Ability Adjustments:'' +1 Intelligence, -1 Charisma
* ''Bonus Feats:'' Talented (any two Knowledge specializations), choose one of Iron Will, Great Fortitude, or Lightning Reflexes
* ''Bonus Skill:'' Citizens of Thalarion gain one bonus known skill at 1st level, in addition to those gained for the hero's role and Intelligence score.
* ''Favored Feats:'' Choose one supernatural power (Citizens of Thalarion treat their total level as their adept level for this power).
