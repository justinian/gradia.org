City of Betrayal
Located in the hills north of the Dragonfangs.

Once known as Muir Doross, the city of gold, the dwarves of this city fell victim to greed and avarice, and fought amongst themselves for many years, to the weakening of their power. The Duergar, called Betrayers or Goblin Dwarves, took over the city  and enslaved it's people. It is not permitted among Dwarves to speak of Muir Duerga, but in the long dirges and ballads that serve as the record of dwarven history, there are traces of memory that a keen ear can still catch.
