The Barony of Dunkirk is a small barony occupying the southwest of Corin. The
barony is well-defended, and often considered a safe haven, far from any
danger. This image is furthered by the people's love for the local ruler, the
Baron John of the House of Dunkirk. The House of Dunkirk were once the noble
house that held the throne in Corin, which ended after the last great king bore
no sons, and the throne was snatched by the House of Pell.

The largest city in the Barony is [Brax Landing]({{< relref "brax-landing.md" >}})
