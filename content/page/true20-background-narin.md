#  Citizen of Narin 
The people of Narin are proud of their rigidly stratified society. Rule of law is all. A Narin citizen knows his place, and no matter how low he is amongst the Narins, he still considers himself better than any outsider. This sense of superiority allows them ease of conscience when wronging those 'lesser' than themselves, either within the Imperial culture or amongst outsiders. Upward mobility is achieved through cunning, patience, and ruthlessness.
 * ''Ability Adjustments:'' None.
* ''Bonus Feats:'' Taunt, Talented (Bluff and Intuition)
* ''Bonus Skill:'' Citizens of Narin gain one bonus known skill at 1st level, in addition to those gained for the hero's role and Intelligence score.
* ''Favored Feats:'' Favored Opponent (Shattered Empire), Master Plan
