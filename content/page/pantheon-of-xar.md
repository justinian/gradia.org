The pantheon of the current ruling faction of the Xar is monotheistic, worshipping Xar, the insect queen who represents the Hive, and its insatiable urge to devour and multiply, until the swarm covers the world.
</p>




But it was not always this way, and the people now known as the Xar once revered different, primative gods that have not been entirely forgotten, and are still worshipped in secret among the Hive.
