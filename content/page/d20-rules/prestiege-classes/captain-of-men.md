The Captain of Men, or just Captain, is a courageous leader of forces, beloved
(or feared) by all who follow him. His will is forged in the furnaces of war,
and he can give his strength to those who follow in his service.

##  Prerequisites 

 * **Base Attack Bonus:** +6
 * **Skills:** Diplomacy 6 ranks, Knowledge (Nobility) 6 ranks or Knowledge
   (Warfare and Tactics) 6
 * **Feats:** Iron Will, Leadership

Must be proficient with all Martial Weapons, must have a leadership rating of
10+

##  Class Features 

 * **Hit Dice:** d10
 * **Class Skills:** Climb, Craft, Diplomacy, Handle Animal, Heal, Jump,
   Knowledge (Nobility), Knowledge (Warfare and Tactics), Perform, Profession,
   Ride, Spot, Wilderness Lore 
 * **Skill Points per Level:** 4 + INT

##  Special Abilities 

 * **Fearlessness** - At 1st level, the Captain gains an Aura of Courage, like
   the Paladin ability.
 * **Leadership Bonus** - At 1st, 4th, 7th, and 10th level, the Captain gets an
   additional +1 bonus to his Leadership rating. This bonus does NOT allow the
   Captain to have a cohort of greater level than himself.
 * **Bonus Feats** - At 2nd, 5th, and 8th level, the Captain gains a bonus
   feat, selected from the Fighter bonus feat list.
 * **Rally** - At 3rd level, the Captain gains the ability, as a move
   equivalent action, to grant all allies within Medium Range (100'+10'/Captain
   Level) who can see and hear him the benefit of the Mobility feat so long as
   they end their turn closer to the Captain than they began. The Captain may
   do this once per day at 3rd Level, 2/day at 6th Level, and 3/day at 9th
   Level. 
 * **Improved Rally** - At 6th level, when the Captain issues a Rally, all
   allies within Medium Range (100'+10'/Captain level) who can see and hear him
   gain the benefits of the Iron Will, Lightning Reflexes, and Iron Will feats,
   and  allies affected by the Rally gain a number of temporary hit points
   equal to 1d4 plus the Captain's Charisma modifier for a number of rounds
   equal to the Captain's class level, so long as they end their turn closer to
   the Captain than they began. 
 * **Supreme Rally** - At 9th level, allies within Close Range (25'+5'/Captain
   Level) who can see and hear the Captain and are affected by the Rally
   recieve a +2 Morale Bonus to Attacks, Armor Class, and Saves,for a number of
   rounds equal to the Captain's Charisma Modifier plus his Captain Level, in
   addition to all other Rally effects. Once within Close Range, all allies
   benefit from all Rally bonuses (so long as they stay in Close Range), for as
   long as the duration lasts.
 * **Training** - At 5th and 8th lvl, the Captain may spend 1 month training
   his followers to give them a bonus feat chosen from Dodge, Expertise,
   Improved Initiative, Improved Unarmed Strike, any Simple or Martial Weapon
   Proficiency, any Armor Proficiency, Mounted Combat, Point Blank Shot, Power
   Attack, and Weapon Focus. The Feat chosen must be one possessed by the
   Captain himself, but the followers ignore the prerequisites for the Feat.
   Different divisions can be taught different feats, but it takes a month for
   each one. After the forces are trained, the experienced followers will train
   any newcomers, taking the burdon of training off their Captain.
 * **Ex-Captains** - Should a Captain's Leadership ever drop below his
   character level +2, he loses the Aura of Courage, Rally, Leadership and
   Training abilities, and may not gain levels as a Captain until his
   leadership score excedes his character level by +4.
