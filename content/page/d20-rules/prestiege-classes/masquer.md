This deceptive class specializes in taking advantage from physical manipulation magic, such as Alter Self, to become powerful spies, infiltrators, and assassins. Much like Doppelgangers, they can slip into the shape of a trusted ally or servent, probe the minds of the weak to uphold their disguise, and shield themselves from detection. Only powerful magics can reveal them, but their power is not limited to deception. At need, they can be powerful physical combatants, as fearsome as they are versitile.

##  Requirements 
**Skills:** Disguise 9 Ranks, Bluff 9 ranks

Base Will Save +4 or higher
* *Special:** The ability to cast Alter Self or Polymorph Self as a spell or a spell-like ability.

##  Class Features 
**Hit Dice:** d6
* *Class Skills:** Alchemy, Appraise, Bluff, Craft, Diplomacy, Disable Device, Gather Information, Hide, Innuendo, Intimidate, Knowledge (Arcana), Listen, Move Silently, Perform, Profession, Search, Sense Motive, Spellcraft, Spot, Tumble, Use Magic Device
* *Skills per level:** 6+INT modifier

##  Special Abilities 
**Weapons and Armor** - The Masquer gains no proficiency in armor or weapons.
* *Shape Self** - The Masquer gains the ability to change her shape, similarly to the Alter Self spell (though not limited to humanoid form), as a supernatural ability. She may do this a number of times per day equal to her Masquer Level plus her Intelligence bonus (if positive). This new form lasts for 1 hour for every level of Masquer the character has, or until dismissed. 


At higher levels, when the Masquer changes shape, she can modify the new form with any of the Modifications she has learned, but each Modification counts as a daily use. For example, Doppell Gangher, a 5th Level Masquer with an Intelligence of 18, has 9 daily Shape Selfs. Doppell runs afoul of an owlbear, and decides that if he was the size of an Owlbear, he could put up a good fight, so he changes his shape to Owlbear, which requires him to increase his size to Large, but gives him +8 Strength, +4 Constitution, and 1 point of Natural Armor (though he loses 1 to AC because of his size). The shapechange costs one use, and he used a modification, so it costs him 2 uses. If he wanted to add natural weapons to that form, it would cost him 3. On the other hand, it takes him just a few moments to kill the stupid creature, but can keep the shape he's in for 5 hours, useful in case this owlbear was hunting with a pack.

Shape Self takes a standard action to use, but assing Modifications do not require extra time.

A Modification can be used alone, without the Masquer spending an extra daily use to change shape. For example, Chan Geling has fallen, unarmed, into a pit with a ravenous dire wolf, but Chan only has one use of Shape Self left. He decides he needs weapons to survive, so he makes claws for his hands. Since he is not changing his shape in any other way, this only requires one use.

All Modifications are supernatural abilities.
 * *Modification (Chameleon)** - The Masquer has the ability to change the color and texture of her outermost layer to match her environment, giving her a +10 circumstance bonus to Hide checks.
* *Still Mind** - At 1st Level, the Masquer has learned to conceal her thoughts, even from magical intrustion. She becomes immune to Detect Thoughts, Analyze Truth, and Know Alignment. This is an extraordinary ability.
* *Modification (Body Arsenal)** - The Masquer can create natural weapons for her new form. Use the natural weapons of the form she adopts, when applicable. Otherwise (if she is creating a new form, or adding weapons to one that does not normally possess them) she can have claws (2 at 1d6 damage each), a bite (1d8), or a gore (with horns for 1d8). When she attains 7th Masquer Level, she may choose a secondary natural attack, which she uses at her highest attack bonus -5, and uses half the Strength modifier to damage. These damages increase when the Masquer's size increases, as detailed in the Monster Manual. The Masquer may optionally give herself a Natural Armor bonus equal to half her Masquer Level rounded down. This bonus stacks with the Size Natural Armor bonus, but no others. Natural weapons and natural armor count as seperate Modifications for purposes of daily uses, and can (but are not required to) be used at the same time.
 * *Detect Thoughts** - At 3rd Level, the Masquer has learned to read the minds of others, casting Detect Thoughts as a spell-like ability as a Sorceror equal to her character level. At 7th Level, she may use this ability 2/day.
* *Modification (Agile Flight)** - The Masquer, when in a form that possesses wings, fly with skill and swiftness. They have an ariel speed of 100ft, and Good manuverability, when using this Modification. Note that they cannot remain airborn if carrying more than their Light Load Capacity, though size and number of legs can increase that. This modification, since it requires wings, cannot be used in a Masquer's natural form unless she possesses wings normally.
* *Nondetection** - At 5th Level, the Masquer has learned how to protect herself from scrying. She can cast Nondetection as a spell-like ability, as a Sorceror equal to her character level. At 9th Level, she may use this ability 2/day.
* *Modification (Quicken Change)** - The Masquer may use the Self-Shape power as a free action. This counts as a Modification for purposes of the daily use limit.
* *Modification (Change Size)** - The Masquer gains the ability to enlarge or reduce her size when using Self-shape. She may increase her size by one level or reduce it by one level. When such changes are made, adjust the character's physical traits, including Reach and Facing according to the size change chart in the Monster Manual.
* *Modification (Shifting Body)** - The Masquer now has such control over her form that she can, when shifting form, grant herself Damage Reduction 5/+1, and becomes immune to critical hits.
