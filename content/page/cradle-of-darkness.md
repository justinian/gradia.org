The Cradle of Darkness is the mountainous peninsula that makes up the southern
shores of the [Lorimar Sea]({{< relref "lorimar-sea.md" >}}). Called the most
treacherous place in all of Gradia, the Cradle is nothing but blasted mountain
ranges, where many active volcanos still exist, occasionally violently making
their presence known.

The Cradle is shrouded in many rumors and legends. It is said that this is
where the [Sun King]({{< relref "sun-king.md" >}}) laid with the tricky and
devious [Sea Queen]({{< relref "sea-queen.md" >}}), and bore the Dark Brood:
the gods of pain and death. (See [Religion]({{< relref "religion.md" >}})) It
is also said that the Cradle of Darkness is the only place left in Gradia that
has a dragon population. Red dragons are said to be numerous here, while
dragons anywhere else in the world are singularities.
