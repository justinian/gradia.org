''My god is greater... He is the everlasting sky. Your god lives beneath him.''

The Four Winds, Father Sky, Lady Night, Stormhammer, and The Soaring One are all names for the infinite sky Heleonir. Worshipped primarily in the coastal areas of Corin and West Narin, Heleonir takes many forms to his/her worshippers. He is masculine by day, and she is feminine at night. He is the lord of the sky, winds and clouds. Hers is the province of the starry night, the evening breeze, the driving rain.

Skywatchers, as priests to the Four Winds are called, do not easily stay in one place, and even the most rooted will venture on long journeys "as the wind takes them." They are taught to be kind of heart, and understanding of other cultures and points of view, though swift in action when action is called for. However, there are as many different approaches to emulating the god as there are worshippers, as each finds their own way to follow the Winds.

Skywatchers scry and divine by watching the open sky. They cannot cast divinations without a view of the sky, be it night or day. They are uncomfortable underground or in doors for extended periods of time.

Skywatchers tend to be reverent towards birds, tending wounded birds they find, and fashioning ornaments and clothing from molted feathers.

Domains: **Travel**, Air, Chaos, Good, and Storm

Favored Weapon: Cloudrender (+2 Thundering Javelin of Shock)
