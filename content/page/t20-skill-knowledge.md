#  Knowledge 
**Ability**: Intelligence
* *Specialized**

The Knowledge skill represents a character's accrued lore on a subject. A Knowledge skill must have an associated specialization, representing the area of study. This skill can be selected multiple times, with each specialization being a separate skill.

Example Specializations:
* Arcana
* Cosmology
* Dungeon
* Engineering
* Geography
* History
* Local
* Nature
* Nobility
* Religion
* Warfare
